## ðŸ”¹ Story Point Estimation
Story points are used to estimate effort using the **Fibonacci series**:

**1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 13**

| Story Points | Effort Estimate |
|-------------|----------------|
| 1 | Few minutes |
| 3 | Few hours |
| 5 | Few days |
| 8 | One week |
| 13 | One month |

> âš ï¸ Story points are based on **effort**, not exact time.
